A New Path Forward

Our political system feels broken because it's designed to extract resources, not empower people. This program follows a proven path to reclaim your agency and build effective, sustainable political power.

Recognize → Understand → Connect → Experiment → Build

What You'll Receive as a Participant

  • 12 weeks of intensive, expert-led virtual sessions designed for real-world application.
  • A dedicated cohort of 25 passionate peers from diverse backgrounds to end political isolation.
  • A comprehensive library of practical tools, templates, and guides you won't find anywhere else.
  • A pre-program "Flight Manual" with foundational resources to help you hit the ground running before Week 1.
  • Hands-on experience developing and launching your own Political Micro-Experiment.
  • Lifetime access to the Political Evolution Project alumni network for ongoing collaboration.

The 12-Week Journey

PHASE 1: RECOGNITION (Weeks 1-3)

"Seeing the Matrix: How Political Extraction Actually Works"

Week 1

Orientation & Political Ecosystem Mapping

Why everything you've been told is wrong and how to map your place in the current system.

Week 2

Following the Money

Decode political finance with an FEC deep dive and learn to read the tea leaves of power.

Week 3

Geographic Extraction

Understand why your community was written off and how "strategic" resource allocation really works.

PHASE 2: UNDERSTANDING (Weeks 4-6)

"The System Behind the System: How Power Really Works"

Week 4

Neoliberalism Demystified

Learn the invisible doctrine that makes everything feel like a business and explore abundance-based alternatives.

Week 5

Power Mapping Like a Pro

Go beyond the obvious players to map who really runs your community and clarify your own theory of change.

Week 6

Modern Organizing Technology

Discover the data and tech tools that actually work, moving your strategy beyond hope and prayers.

PHASE 3: CONNECTION (Weeks 7-8)

"Finding Your People: Building Coalition Across Traditional Boundaries"

Week 7

Finding Strange Bedfellows: The Art of Issue-Based Coalitions

A deep dive into building effective alliances, focusing on shared goals and strategic collaboration.

Week 8

Community Building & Sustainable Organizing

Learn anti-exploitation practices to build movements and community without burning out.

PHASE 4: EXPERIMENTATION (Weeks 9-10)

"Testing New Models: What Works in the Real World"

Week 9

Designing Your Political Micro-Experiment

Develop a small-scale, testable action to apply your learnings and generate new insights.

Week 10

Implementation & Troubleshooting

Move your plan to action with practical strategies for implementation and problem-solving.

PHASE 5: BUILDING (Weeks 11-12)

"Creating the Future: Sustainable Change That Lasts"

Week 11

Progress Reports & Iteration

Learn from doing by honestly assessing progress and adjusting your strategy based on real-world feedback.

Week 12

Celebration & Future Planning

Showcase the learnings from your micro-experiment and chart the course for your (and our) next steps.

Meet Your Facilitator

Carrie Clendening

Carrie is a political educator and strategist who went from being a nonpartisan observer to a "political education dealer." Frustrated by an ecosystem that thrives on exploiting the passion of organizers and activists, she created the Political Evolution Project to equip a new generation of leaders with the practical skills and strategic understanding the establishment won't teach them. Her work is informed by years of experience in Charleston, WV and beyond, learning from both allies and adversaries what it truly takes to build lasting power.
