Why This Program Exists

Let's be brutally honest: The current political system isn't broken—it's working exactly as designed. It's a resource extraction operation that mines human hope like Bitcoin, leaving organizers exhausted and communities powerless while consultants cash checks.

"After watching too many brilliant organizers burn out trying to fix a system that feeds on their exhaustion, I realized we don't need better tactics. We need a whole new species of political engagement."

This program emerged from a simple observation: The most effective political operators aren't the ones shouting the loudest or working the hardest. They're the ones who understand the ecosystem well enough to hack it. Think of it as political biomimicry—learning from the organisms that have already evolved past predation.

We're not here to teach you how to be a better cog in the machine. We're here to show you how to build entirely new machines. Or better yet, organic systems that don't need machines at all.

The 12-Week Metamorphosis

Each phase of this journey is designed to systematically deprogram you from extraction-based politics and rebuild your understanding from the cellular level up. Think of it as a political detox followed by strategic steroids.

PHASE 1: RECOGNITION (Weeks 1-3)

"Red Pill Time: Seeing the Extraction Matrix"

Week 1

The Great Political Unplugging

We start by torching everything you think you know about politics. Map your position in the extraction ecosystem and discover why your well-meaning efforts keep feeding the beast you're trying to slay. Spoiler: The call is coming from inside the house.

Week 2

Follow the Money (With Forensic Accounting)

Deep dive into FEC data like we're auditing the Matrix itself. Learn to read political finance reports like tea leaves that actually predict the future. By the end, you'll spot dark money patterns faster than a Bloomberg terminal.

Week 3

The Geography of Abandonment

Decode why your community became a "sacrifice zone" in the grand political chess game. We'll map the extraction patterns that turned your hometown into a resource colony for coastal consultants. (Hint: It's not an accident.)

PHASE 2: UNDERSTANDING (Weeks 4-6)

"The Operating System: How Power Actually Compiles"

Week 4

Neoliberalism: The Invisible OS Running Everything

Finally understand why everything feels like a LinkedIn post written by a venture capitalist. We'll decode the economic theology that turned citizens into customers and democracy into a market failure. Plus: abundance-based alternatives that don't suck.

Week 5

Power Mapping Like a CIA Analyst

Move beyond the obvious puppets to identify the actual puppet masters. Build multidimensional power maps that would make string theory physicists jealous. Bonus: Clarify your own theory of change (beyond "hope something works").

Week 6

Tech Stack for the Revolution

Discover the actual tools that work, not the ones sold by "political tech" grifters. From data management to distributed organizing, we'll build your technical arsenal. Warning: May cause allergic reactions to NGP VAN.

PHASE 3: CONNECTION (Weeks 7-8)

"Pack Formation: Building Your Political Wolf Pack"

Week 7

Strange Bedfellows & Strategic Alliances

Learn to build coalitions that would make The Avengers look homogeneous. Master the art of finding common ground with people you'd normally block on Twitter. Sometimes your best ally is the enemy of your enemy's enemy. (Yes, the math checks out.)

Week 8

Sustainable Organizing (Not an Oxymoron)

Build movements that don't cannibalize their own organizers. Learn anti-extraction practices that create energy instead of depleting it. Think permaculture principles applied to political organizing. Your burnout is not a badge of honor.

PHASE 4: EXPERIMENTATION (Weeks 9-10)

"Beta Testing Democracy: Your Political Petri Dish"

Week 9

Designing Your Political Micro-Experiment

Time to get your hands dirty. Design a small-scale intervention that tests your new understanding. Think "minimum viable campaign" meets "chaos theory." We'll help you craft something that's ambitious enough to matter but small enough to actually work.

Week 10

Deployment & Debugging

Launch your experiment into the wild and learn to troubleshoot in real-time. This is where theory meets the beautiful messiness of actual humans. Expect plot twists. Embrace the chaos. Document everything.

PHASE 5: BUILDING (Weeks 11-12)

"Emergence: From Experiments to Ecosystems"

Week 11

Autopsy of an Experiment

Conduct a forensic analysis of what worked, what exploded, and what surprised you. Learn the art of failing forward fast. This is where we separate correlation from causation and hope from strategy.

Week 12

From Prototype to Ecosystem

Present your findings to the hive mind and collectively chart next steps. This isn't a graduation—it's a beginning. Leave with concrete plans to scale what works and a network that actually has your back.
